# Delete Account & Data — True Count: Blackjack Trainer

**Effective date:** February 19, 2026

This page explains how you can request deletion of your account and associated data for **True Count: Blackjack Trainer** (the “App”).

## Option 1: Delete from within the App (if available)

If the App includes an in-app “Delete Account” option:

1. Open the App
2. Go to **Online Leaderboards**
4. Tap **Delete Account**
5. Confirm deletion

## Option 2: Request deletion by email

If you do not see an in-app delete option, you can request deletion by email:

Email: **[truecounttrainer@gmail.com](mailto:truecounttrainer@gmail.com)**
Subject: **Delete my True Count: Blackjack Trainer account**

Include:

* The sign-in method you used (**Google / Apple / Email / Anonymous**)
* If you signed in with email, include the **email address** used
* If you used Google/Apple, include the **display name** (if any) and the approximate date you created the account (optional)

## What will be deleted

When we delete your account, we will delete or de-identify:

* Your authentication account record (Firebase Authentication identifier)
* Any cloud-stored user data associated with your account (if applicable, such as leaderboard/profile records)

## What is not automatically deleted

* **Data stored locally on your device** (training stats, settings, progress) is not accessible to us remotely.
  To remove local data, uninstall the App or clear the App’s storage in your device settings.

## Timeframe

We aim to complete verified deletion requests within **30 days**.
